Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: April 30, 2026

🔍 Quick Summary: Some links on AI Tool Finder are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. This does not affect our ratings or recommendations.

How We Make Money

AI Tool Finder is a free resource. We invest significant time and effort into testing AI tools, writing detailed reviews, and maintaining comparison data. To sustain this work, we participate in affiliate programs offered by some of the tools we review.

Here's how it works:

  1. We review and rate AI tools based on hands-on testing — not affiliate potential.
  2. When a tool offers an affiliate program, we apply and — if accepted — use affiliate links on relevant pages.
  3. If you click an affiliate link and sign up or subscribe, we may earn a commission. You pay the same price (or sometimes less, if we negotiate a deal).

Our Commitment to Honesty

We never let affiliate relationships influence our ratings, rankings, or recommendations. Our review process is independent:

  • We test every tool ourselves before reviewing.
  • Stars and rankings come from testing criteria, not commission rates.
  • We don't accept payment for positive reviews.
  • If a tool is bad, we say so — affiliate link or not.

Affiliate Programs We Participate In

As of April 2026, we are members of the following affiliate programs:

  • PartnerStack — Notion, Jasper, and select SaaS tools
  • ShareASale — Various AI and SaaS products
  • Impact / Impact Radius — Enterprise AI platforms
  • Direct affiliate programs — Individual tool partnerships

Not all tools we review have affiliate links. We only use them where available and relevant.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure complies with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) Endorsement Guides (16 CFR Part 255). Every page containing affiliate links includes a visible disclosure notice. Individual affiliate links are marked with rel="nofollow sponsored" attributes as recommended by Google.
