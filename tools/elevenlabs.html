Frequently Asked Questions

Is ElevenLabs free? ▼
Yes—ElevenLabs offers a free tier with 10,000 characters per month (~10 minutes of audio) and access to pre-made voices. Paid plans: Starter ($5/mo, 30K chars), Creator ($22/mo, 100K chars + voice cloning), Pro ($99/mo, 500K chars), Scale ($330/mo, 2M chars).
Can ElevenLabs clone any voice? ▼
Voice cloning requires a voice sample (30 seconds for Instant, 30+ minutes for Professional quality). ElevenLabs requires consent verification—you must confirm you have permission to clone the voice. The platform actively prevents unauthorized cloning of public figures.
How realistic are ElevenLabs voices? ▼
Extremely realistic—blind listening tests routinely show listeners cannot distinguish ElevenLabs TTS from human recordings. The 2025 Turbo v2.5 model added emotional inflection, natural pausing, and breath sounds that dramatically increased realism.
Can ElevenLabs dub videos into other languages? ▼
Yes—ElevenLabs Dubbing Studio translates and dubs content into 29 languages while preserving the original speaker's voice characteristics, emotional tone, and timing. It handles uploaded video/audio files and provides timeline editing for fine-tuning.
